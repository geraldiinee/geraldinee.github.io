The college of Criminal Justice Education through the criminology students' society conducted 1 day seminar on Forensic Ballestics to Criminology Students on April 27, 2023 at the Balanghaw Hall with the theme: New Forensic Ballistic Foundation Techniques aa Foundation for Detection and Investigation".
This program aimed to provide the criminology students the necessary knowledge and understanding on Forensic Ballistic in relation to investigation and crime detection that are essential for their future endeavors. In the afternoon the criminology students undergone practical exercises in Bullet Marketing/Striation,after that 1st year to 4th year representatives gives thier impression on what they learned followed by a closing remarks from the program head Raymond Rolin D. Hilado.
The guest speaker of the program is from the Negros Occidental Provincial Forensic Unit Unit PSSg Arnel L. Dela Cruz, Ballistician and PCpl Jolibert A. Vingco, fingerprint expert who shared their expertise valuable lesson and give motivitional to future law future enforcer.
